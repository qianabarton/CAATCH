CAATCH (Celebrating African Americans, The Culture and History) is a week long celebration during Black history month.

CAATCH
UP!

CAATCH uses this celebration during Black history month as a common denominator to unite and connect important and vital segments of the African American community.

CAATCH
US!

What are these segments? The Church, Education, our prestige organization(divine nine, others), our media(print, digital, and others) Activist(NAN, Black lives matters), Black businesses, The arts & culture(music, etc), our financial institutions, Doctors(medical, philosophy, psychologists, etc), Security, lawyers, and Rites of passage & other child development programs United by love of self. (remembering, ‘we don’t have to hate anybody to love ourselves’) Let’s celebrate Black history the right way!
